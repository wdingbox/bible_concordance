  #6424 פָּלַס palac {paw-las'}

 a primitive root; TWOT - 1777; v

—Hebrew Word Study (Transliteration-Pronunciation Etymology & Grammar)


 1) (Piel) to weigh, make level, balance
    1a) to weigh out
    1b) to make level or smooth

—Brown-Driver-Briggs (Old Testament Hebrew-English Lexicon)

A primitive root; properly to roll flat, that is, prepare (a road); also to revolve, that is, weigh (mentally):—make, ponder, weigh.

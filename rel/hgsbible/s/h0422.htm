  #0422 אָלָה 'alah {aw-law'}

 a primitive root; TWOT - 94; v

—Hebrew Word Study (Transliteration-Pronunciation Etymology & Grammar)


 1) to swear, curse
    1a) (Qal)
        1a1) to swear, take an oath (before God)
        1a2) to curse
    1b) (Hiphil)
        1b1) to put under oath, adjure
        1b2) to put under a curse

—Brown-Driver-Briggs (Old Testament Hebrew-English Lexicon)

A primitive root; properly to adjure, that is, (usually in a bad sense) imprecate:—adjure, curse, swear.

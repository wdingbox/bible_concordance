  #3341 יָצַת yatsath {yaw-tsath'}

 a primitive root; TWOT - 899; v

—Hebrew Word Study (Transliteration-Pronunciation Etymology & Grammar)


 1) to kindle, burn, set on fire
    1a) (Qal) to kindle
    1b) (Niphal)
        1b1) to be kindled
        1b2) to be left desolate
    1c) (Hiphil) to set on fire, kindle

—Brown-Driver-Briggs (Old Testament Hebrew-English Lexicon)

A primitive root; to burn or set on fire; figuratively to desolate:—burn (up), be desolate, set (on) fire ([fire]), kindle.

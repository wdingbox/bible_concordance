  #2425 ἱκανός hikanos {hik-an-os'}

 from hiko [hikano or hikneomai, akin to G2240] (to arrive);
    TDNT - 3:293,361; adj

—Greek Word Study (Transliteration-Pronunciation Etymology & Grammar)


 1) sufficient
    1a) many enough, enough
    1b) sufficient in ability, i.e. meet, fit

—Thayer's (New Testament Greek-English Lexicon)

From ἵκω [[hiko]] (ἱκάνω [[hikano]] or ἱκνέομαι [[hikneomai]]; akin to G2240; to arrive); competent (as if coming in season), that is, ample (in amount) or fit (in character):—able, + content, enough, good, great, large, long (while), many, meet, much, security, sore, sufficient, worthy.

  #0952 βέβηλος bebelos {beb'-ay-los}

 from the base of G0939 and belos (a threshold); TDNT - 1:604,104; adj

—Greek Word Study (Transliteration-Pronunciation Etymology & Grammar)


 1) accessible, lawful to be trodden
    1a) of places
 2) profane
    2a) unhallowed, common, public place
    2b) of men, ungodly

—Thayer's (New Testament Greek-English Lexicon)

From the base of G0939 and βηλός [[belos]] (a threshold); accessible (as by crossing the door way), that is, (by implication of Jewish notions) heathenish, wicked:—profane (person).

  #4279 מָחָר machar {maw-khar'}

 probably from H0309; TWOT - 1185a; n m

—Hebrew Word Study (Transliteration-Pronunciation Etymology & Grammar)


 1) tomorrow, in time to come, in the future
    1a) tomorrow (as the day following the present day)
    1b) in future time

—Brown-Driver-Briggs (Old Testament Hebrew-English Lexicon)

Probably from H0309; properly deferred, that is, the morrow; usually (adverbially) tomorrow; indefinitely hereafter:—time to come, tomorrow.

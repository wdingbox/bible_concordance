  #0818 ἀτιμάζω atimazo {at-im-ad'-zo}

 from G0820; TDNT - n/a; v

—Greek Word Study (Transliteration-Pronunciation Etymology & Grammar)


 1) to dishonour, insult, treat with contempt
    1a) whether in word, deed or thought

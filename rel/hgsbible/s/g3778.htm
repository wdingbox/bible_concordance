Including the nominative masculine plural (second form), nominative feminine signular (third form), and the nominate feminine plural, (fourth form). From the article G3588 and G0846; the he (she or it), that is, this or that (often with the article repeated):—he (it was that), hereof, it, she, such as, the same, these, they, this (man, same, woman), which, who.

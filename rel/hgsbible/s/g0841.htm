  #0841 αὐτάρκεια autarkeia {ow-tar'-ki-ah}

 from G0842; TDNT - 1:466,78; n f

—Greek Word Study (Transliteration-Pronunciation Etymology & Grammar)


 1) a perfect condition of life in which no aid or support is needed
 2) sufficiency of the necessities of life
 3) a mind contented with its lot, contentment

—Thayer's (New Testament Greek-English Lexicon)

From G0842; self satisfaction, that is, (abstractly) contentedness, or (concretely) a competence:—contentment, sufficiency.

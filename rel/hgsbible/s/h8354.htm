  #8354 שָׁתָה shathah {shaw-thaw'}

 a primitive root; TWOT - 2477; v

—Hebrew Word Study (Transliteration-Pronunciation Etymology & Grammar)


 1) to drink
    1a) (Qal)
        1a1) to drink
             1a1a) of drinking cup of God's wrath, of slaughter, of
                   wicked deeds (fig)
        1a2) to feast
    1b) (Niphal) to be drunk

—Brown-Driver-Briggs (Old Testament Hebrew-English Lexicon)

A primitive root; to imbibe (literally or figuratively):— X assuredly, banquet, X certainly, drink (-er, -ing), drunk (X -ard), surely. [Prop. intensive of H8248.]

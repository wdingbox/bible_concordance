  #5039 τεκμήριον tekmerion {tek-may'-ree-on}

 from a presumed derivative of tekmar (a goal or fixed limit); TDNT - n/a; n n

—Greek Word Study (Transliteration-Pronunciation Etymology & Grammar)


 1) that from which something is surely and plainly known
 2) on indubitable evidence, a proof

—Thayer's (New Testament Greek-English Lexicon)

Neuter of a presumed derivative of τεκμάρ [[tekmar]] (a goal or fixed limit); a token (as defining a fact), that is, criterion of certainty:—infallible proof.

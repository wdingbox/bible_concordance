  #3096 יַהַץ Yahats {yah'-hats}
     or ַיַהְצָה Yahtsah {yah'-tsaw}
     or (fem.) יָהְצָה Yahtsah {yah-tsaw'}

 from an unused root meaning to stamp; TWOT - n/a; n pr loc

    king of the Amorites; site uncertain

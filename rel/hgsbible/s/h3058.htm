  #3058 יֵהוּא Yehuw' {yay-hoo'}

 from H3068 and H1931; TWOT - n/a; n pr m

—Hebrew Word Study (Transliteration-Pronunciation Etymology & Grammar)


 Jehu = "Jehovah is He"
 1) the king of the northern kingdom Israel who overthrew the dynasty
    of Omri
 2) son of Hanani and an Israelite prophet in the time of Baasha and
    Jehoshaphat
 3) the Antothite, a Benjamite, one of David's mighty warriors
 4) a descendant of Judah of the house of Hezron
 5) son of Josibiah and a chief of the tribe of Simeon

—Brown-Driver-Briggs (Old Testament Hebrew-English Lexicon)

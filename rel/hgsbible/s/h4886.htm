  #4886 משׁח mashach {maw-shakh'}

 a primitive root; TWOT - 1255; v

—Hebrew Word Study (Transliteration-Pronunciation Etymology & Grammar)


 1) to smear, anoint, spread a liquid
    1a) (Qal)
        1a1) to smear
        1a2) to anoint (as consecration)
        1a3) to anoint, consecrate
    1b) (Niphal) to be anointed

—Brown-Driver-Briggs (Old Testament Hebrew-English Lexicon)

A primitive root; to rub with oil, that is, to anoint; by implication to consecrate; also to paint:—anoint, paint.

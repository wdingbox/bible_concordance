  #7237 רַבָּה Rabbah {rab-baw'}

 fem. of H7227; TWOT - n/a; n pr loc

—Hebrew Word Study (Transliteration-Pronunciation Etymology & Grammar)


 Rabbath = "great"
 1) the capital city of the Ammonites located east of the Jordan
 2) a town in Judah; site uncertain

—Brown-Driver-Briggs (Old Testament Hebrew-English Lexicon)

Feminine of H7227; great; Rabbah, the name of two places in Palestine, East and West:—Rabbah, Rabbath.

  #6963 קוֹל qowl {kole}
     or קֹל qol {kole}

 from an unused root meaning to call aloud; TWOT - 1998a,2028b; n m

—Hebrew Word Study (Transliteration-Pronunciation Etymology & Grammar)


 1) voice, sound, noise
    1a) voice
    1b) sound (of instrument)
 2) lightness, frivolity

  #1705 ἐμπί[μ]πλημι empiplemi {em-pip'-lay-mee}
     or ἐμπλήθω empletho {em-play'-tho}

 from G1722 and the base of G4118; TDNT - 6:128,840; v

—Greek Word Study (Transliteration-Pronunciation Etymology & Grammar)


 1) to fill up, fill full
 2) to take one's fill of, glut one's desire for, satisfy, satiate

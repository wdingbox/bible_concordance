  #0123 אֱדֹם 'Edom {ed-ome'}
     or (fully) אֱדוֹם 'Edowm {ed-ome'}

 from H0122; TWOT - 26e; n pr m

—Hebrew Word Study (Transliteration-Pronunciation Etymology & Grammar)


 Edom = "red"
 1) Edom
 2) Edomite, Idumean - descendants of Esau
 3) land of Edom, Idumea - land south and south east of Palestine

—Brown-Driver-Briggs (Old Testament Hebrew-English Lexicon)

From H0122; red (see Genesis 25:25); Edom, the elder twin-brother of Jacob; hence the region (Idumaea) occuped by him:—Edom, Edomites, Idumea.

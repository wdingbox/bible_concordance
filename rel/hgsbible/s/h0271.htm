  #0271 אָחָז 'Achaz {aw-khawz'}

 from H0270; TWOT - n/a; n pr m

—Hebrew Word Study (Transliteration-Pronunciation Etymology & Grammar)


 Ahaz = "he has grasped"
 1) king of Judah, son Jotham, father of Hezekiah
 2) a Benjamite, son of Micah, and great grandson of Jonathan

—Brown-Driver-Briggs (Old Testament Hebrew-English Lexicon)

From H0270; possessor; Achaz, the name of a Jewish king and of an Israelite:—Ahaz.

  #4013 περιάγω periago {per-ee-ag'-o}

 from G4012 and G0071; TDNT - n/a; v

—Greek Word Study (Transliteration-Pronunciation Etymology & Grammar)


 1) to lead around, to lead about with one's self
 2) to go about, walk about

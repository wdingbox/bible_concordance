  #1968 הֵימָן Heyman {hay-mawn'}

 probably from H0539; TWOT - n/a; n pr m

—Hebrew Word Study (Transliteration-Pronunciation Etymology & Grammar)


 Heman = "faithful"
 1) a wise man to whom Solomon was compared
 2) son of Joel, grandson of Samuel, and a Levitical singer and author
    of PS 88
 3) a seer

—Brown-Driver-Briggs (Old Testament Hebrew-English Lexicon)

Probably from H0530; faithful; Heman, the name of at least two Israelites:—Heman.

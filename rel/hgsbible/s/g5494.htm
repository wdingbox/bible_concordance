  #5494 χειμών cheimon {khi-mone'}

 from a derivative of cheo (to pour, akin to the base of G5490 through

 1) winter
    1a) stormy or rainy weather, a tempest
    1b) winter, the winter season

—Thayer's (New Testament Greek-English Lexicon)

From a derivation of χέω [[cheo]] (to pour; akin to the base of G5490 through the idea of a channel), meaning a storm (as pouring rain); by implication the rainy season, that is, winter:—tempest, foul weather, winter.

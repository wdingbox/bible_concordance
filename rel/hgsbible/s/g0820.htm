  #0820 ἄτιμος atimos {at'-ee-mos}

 from G0001 (as a negative particle) and G5092; TDNT - n/a; adj

—Greek Word Study (Transliteration-Pronunciation Etymology & Grammar)


 1) without honour, unhonoured, dishonoured
 2) base, of less esteem

—Thayer's (New Testament Greek-English Lexicon)

From G0001 (as a negative particle) and G5092; (negatively) unhonoured or (positively) dishonoured. May show a comparative degree such as less honourable:—despised, without honour, less honourable [comparative degree].

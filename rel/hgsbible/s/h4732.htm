  #4732 מִקְלוֹת Miqlowth {mik-lohth'} (or perhaps מִכְּלוֹת Mikk@lowth {mik-kel-ohth'})

 plural of (feminine) H4731; TWOT - n/a; n pr m

—Hebrew Word Study (Transliteration-Pronunciation Etymology & Grammar)


 Mikloth = "staves"
 1) one of the sons of Jehiel, the father or prince of Gibeon by his
         wife Maachah; a Benjamite
 2) leader of the 2nd division of David's army

—Brown-Driver-Briggs (Old Testament Hebrew-English Lexicon)

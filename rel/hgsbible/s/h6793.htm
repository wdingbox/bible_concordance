  #6793 צִנָּה tsinnah {tsin-naw'}

 from H6791; TWOT - 1936b,1937a,1938a; n f

—Hebrew Word Study (Transliteration-Pronunciation Etymology & Grammar)


 1) something piercing, hook, barb
    1a) meaning dubious
 2) coolness, cold (of snow)
 3) shield, large shield, buckler
    3a) shield

—Brown-Driver-Briggs (Old Testament Hebrew-English Lexicon)

Feminine of H6791; a hook (as pointed); also a (large) shield (as if guarding by prickliness); also cold (as piercing):—buckler, cold, hook, shield, target.

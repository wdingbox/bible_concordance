  #3760 כַּרְמֶל Karmel {kar-mel'}

 the same as H3759; TWOT - 1042; n pr loc

—Hebrew Word Study (Transliteration-Pronunciation Etymology & Grammar)


 Carmel = "garden-land"
 1) a mountain on the Mediterranean coast of northern Israel, just
    below Haifa
 2) a town in the mountains on the west side of the Dead Sea and south
    of Hebron

—Brown-Driver-Briggs (Old Testament Hebrew-English Lexicon)

The same as H3759; Karmel, the name of a hill and of a town in Palestine:—Carmel, fruitful (plentiful) field, (place).

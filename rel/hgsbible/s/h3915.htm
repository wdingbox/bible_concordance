  #3915 לַיִל layil {lah'-yil}
     or (Isa. 21:11) לֵיל leyl {lale}

—Hebrew Word Study (Transliteration-Pronunciation Etymology & Grammar)


 1) night
    1a) night (as opposed to day)
    1b) of gloom, protective shadow (fig.)

  #3003 יָבֵישׁ Yabesh {yaw-bashe'}

 the same as H3002 (also Yabeysh {yaw-bashe'}); TWOT - n/a;

—Hebrew Word Study (Transliteration-Pronunciation Etymology & Grammar)


 Jabesh = "dry"
 n pr loc
 1) a town in the territory of Gilead; according to Eusebius, it is
    beyond the Jordan, 6 miles (10 km) from Pella, on the mountain
    road to Gerasa; site unknown but may be modern 'Wadi Yabes'
 n pr m
 2) father of Shallum, the 15th king of the northern kingdom

—Brown-Driver-Briggs (Old Testament Hebrew-English Lexicon)

The same as H3002. (Also, often with the addition of H1568, that is, Jabesh of Gilad); Jabesh, the name of an Israelite and of a place in Palestine:—Jabesh ([-Gilead]).

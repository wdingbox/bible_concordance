  #5110 τόκος tokos {tok'-os}

 from the base of G5088; TDNT - n/a; n m

—Greek Word Study (Transliteration-Pronunciation Etymology & Grammar)


 1) birth
    1a) the act of bringing forth
    1b) that which has been brought forth, offspring
 2) interest of money, usury (because it multiplies money, and as it
    were "breeds")

  #0256 אַחְאָב 'Ach'ab {akh-awb'}
  once (by contraction) 'Echab (Jer. 29:22) {ekh-awb'}

 from H0251 and H0001; n pr m

—Hebrew Word Study (Transliteration-Pronunciation Etymology & Grammar)


 Ahab = "father's brother"
 1) king of Israel, son of Omri, husband of Jezebel
 2) false prophet executed by Nebuchadrezzar, time of Jeremiah

—Brown-Driver-Briggs (Old Testament Hebrew-English Lexicon)

The second form used once (by contraction) in Jeremiah 29:22; from H0251 and H0001; brother (that is, friend) of (his) father; Achab, the name of a king of Israel and of a prophet at Babylon:—Ahab.

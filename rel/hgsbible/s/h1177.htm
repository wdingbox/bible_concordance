  #1177 בַּעַל חָנָן Ba`al Chanan {bah'-al khaw-nawn'}

 from H1167 and H2603; TWOT - n/a; n pr m

—Hebrew Word Study (Transliteration-Pronunciation Etymology & Grammar)


 Baal-hanan = "Baal is gracious"
 1) an early king of Edom
 2) one of David's officers, a Gederite, who had the superintendence
    of his olive and sycamore plantations

—Brown-Driver-Briggs (Old Testament Hebrew-English Lexicon)

From H1167 and H2608; possessor of grace; Baal Chanan, the name of an Edomite, also of an Israelite:—Baal-hanan.

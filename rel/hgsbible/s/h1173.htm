—Hebrew Word Study (Transliteration-Pronunciation Etymology & Grammar)


 Baalah = "mistress"
 1) another name for Kirjath-jearim or Kirjath-baal; modern Kuriet el Enab
 2) a town in the south of Judah, also called Balah and Bilhah

  #0914 בָּדַל badal {baw-dal'}

 a primitive root; TWOT - 203; v

—Hebrew Word Study (Transliteration-Pronunciation Etymology & Grammar)


 1) to divide, separate
    1a) (Hiphil)
        1a1) to divide, separate, sever
        1a2) to separate, set apart
        1a3) to make a distinction, difference
        1a4) to divide into parts
    1b) (Niphal)
        1b1) to separate oneself from (reflexive of 1a2)
        1b2) to withdraw from
        1b3) to separate oneself unto
        1b4) to be separated
        1b5) to be excluded
        1b6) to be set apart

A primitive root; to divide (in various senses literally or figuratively, separate, distinguish, differ, select, etc.):—(make, put) difference, divide (asunder), (make) separate (self, -ation), sever (out), X utterly.

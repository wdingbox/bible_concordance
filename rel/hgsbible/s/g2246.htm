  #2246 ἥλιος helios {hay'-lee-os}

 from hele (a ray, perhaps akin to the alternate of G0138); TDNT - n/a; n m

—Greek Word Study (Transliteration-Pronunciation Etymology & Grammar)


 1) the sun
 2) the rays of the sun
 3) the light of day

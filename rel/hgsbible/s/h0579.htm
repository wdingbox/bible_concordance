  #0579 אָנַה 'anah {aw-naw'}

 a primitive root [perhaps rather identical with H0578 through the
    idea of contraction in anguish]; TWOT - 126; v

—Hebrew Word Study (Transliteration-Pronunciation Etymology & Grammar)


 1) to meet, encounter, approach, be opportune
    1a) (Piel) to allow to meet, cause to meet
    1b) (Pual) to be sent, be allowed to meet
    1c) (Hithpael) to seek occasion (quarrel), cause oneself to meet

—Brown-Driver-Briggs (Old Testament Hebrew-English Lexicon)

A primitive root (perhaps rather identical with H0578 through the idea of contraction in anguish); to approach; hence to meet in various senses:—befall, deliver, happen, seek a quarrel.

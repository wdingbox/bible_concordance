  #2833 κνήθω knetho {knay'-tho}

 from a primary knao (to scrape); TDNT - n/a; v

—Greek Word Study (Transliteration-Pronunciation Etymology & Grammar)


 1) to scratch, tickle, make to itch
 2) to itch
 3) desirous of hearing something pleasant

From a primary word κνάω [[knao]] (to scrape); to scratch, that is, (by implication) to tickle:— X itching.

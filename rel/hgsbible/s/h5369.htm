—Hebrew Word Study (Transliteration-Pronunciation Etymology & Grammar)


 Ner = "lamp"
 1) son of Jehiel, father of Kish, and grandfather of king Saul
 2) father of Abner, the uncle of Saul
    2a) perhaps the same as 1

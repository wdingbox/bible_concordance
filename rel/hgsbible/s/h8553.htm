  #8553 תִּמְנָה Timnah {tim-naw'}

 from H4487; TWOT - n/a; n pr loc

—Hebrew Word Study (Transliteration-Pronunciation Etymology & Grammar)


 Timnath or Timnah or Thimnathah = "portion"
 1) a town on the northern boundary of Judah later assigned to Dan
 2) a town in the hill country of Judah

—Brown-Driver-Briggs (Old Testament Hebrew-English Lexicon)

From H4487; a portion assigned; Timnah, the name of two places in Palestine:—Timnah, Timnath, Thimnathah.

—Hebrew Word Study (Transliteration-Pronunciation Etymology & Grammar)


 Asshur or Assyria = "a step"
 n pr m
 1) the second son of Shem, eponymous ancestor of the Assyrians
 2) the people of Assyria
 n pr loc
 3) the nation, Assyria
 4) the land, Assyria or Asshur

—Brown-Driver-Briggs (Old Testament Hebrew-English Lexicon)

Apparently from H0833 (in the sense of successful); Ashshur, the second son of Shem; also his descendants and the country occupied by them (that is, Assyria), its region and its empire:—Asshur, Assur, Assyria, Assyrians. See H0838.

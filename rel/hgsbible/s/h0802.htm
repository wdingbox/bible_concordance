  #0802 אִשָּׁה 'ishshah {ish-shaw'}

 from H0376 or H0582; TWOT - 137a; n f

—Hebrew Word Study (Transliteration-Pronunciation Etymology & Grammar)


 1) woman, wife, female
    1a) woman (opposite of man)
    1b) wife (woman married to a man)
    1c) female (of animals)
    1d) each, every (pronoun)

—Brown-Driver-Briggs (Old Testament Hebrew-English Lexicon)

The first form is the feminine of H0376 or H0582; the second form is an irregular plural; a woman (used in the same wide sense as H0582).:—[adulter]ess, each, every, female, X many, + none, one, + together, wife, woman. Often unexpressed in English.

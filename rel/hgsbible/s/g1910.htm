  #1910 ἐπιβαίνω epibaino {ep-ee-bah'-ee-no}

 from G1909 and the base of G0939; TDNT - n/a; v

—Greek Word Study (Transliteration-Pronunciation Etymology & Grammar)


 1) to get upon, mount
    1a) to embark in
    1b) to go aboard (a ship)
 2) to set foot in, enter

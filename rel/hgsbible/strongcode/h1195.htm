  #1195 בַּעֲנָה Ba`ana' {bah-an-aw'}

 the same as H1196; TWOT - n/a; n pr m

—Hebrew Word Study (Transliteration-Pronunciation Etymology & Grammar)


 Baana or Baanah = "in the affliction"
 1) the son of Ahilud, Solomon's commissariat officer in Jezreel and
    the north of the Jordan valley
 2) another of Solomon's officers
 3) father of Zadok who assisted in rebuilding the wall of Jerusalem
    under Nehemiah

—Brown-Driver-Briggs (Old Testament Hebrew-English Lexicon)

 a primitive root; TWOT - 980; v

—Hebrew Word Study (Transliteration-Pronunciation Etymology & Grammar)


 1) to restrict, restrain, withhold, shut up, keep back, refrain, forbid
    1a) (Qal)

        1a3) to withhold

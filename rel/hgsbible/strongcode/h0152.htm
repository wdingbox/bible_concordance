  #0152 אַדְרַמֶּלֶךְ 'Adrammelek {ad-ram-meh'-lek}

 from H0142 and H4428; TWOT - n/a; n pr m

—Hebrew Word Study (Transliteration-Pronunciation Etymology & Grammar)


 Adrammelech = "honour of the king"
            or "Adar is prince"
            or "Adar is Counsellor, Decider"
 1) an idol or god of the Sepharvites, introduced to Israel by
    Shalmaneser the fifth
 2) the son and murderer of Sennacherib

—Brown-Driver-Briggs (Old Testament Hebrew-English Lexicon)

From H0142 and H4428; splendor of (the) king; Adrammelek, the name of an Assyrian idol, also of a son of Sennacherib:—Adrammelech.

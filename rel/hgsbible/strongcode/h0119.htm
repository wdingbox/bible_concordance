  #0119 אָדַם 'adam {aw-dam'}

 of unknown derivation; TWOT - 26b; v

—Hebrew Word Study (Transliteration-Pronunciation Etymology & Grammar)


 1) to be red, red
    1a) (Qal) ruddy (of Nazarites)
    1b) (Pual)
        1b1) to be rubbed red
        1b2) dyed red
        1b3) reddened
    1c) (Hiphil)
        1c1) to cause to show red
        1c2) to glare
        1c3) to emit (show) redness
    1d) (Hithpael)
        1d1) to redden
        1d2) to grow red
        1d3) to look red

—Brown-Driver-Briggs (Old Testament Hebrew-English Lexicon)

To show blood (in the face), that is, flush or turn rosy:—be (dyed, made) red (ruddy).

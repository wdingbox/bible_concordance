  #4453 πωλέω poleo {po-leh'-o}

 probably ultimately from pelomai (to be busy, to trade); TDNT - n/a; v

—Greek Word Study (Transliteration-Pronunciation Etymology & Grammar)


 1) to barter, to sell
 2) sellers

—Thayer's (New Testament Greek-English Lexicon)

Probably ultimately from πέλομαι [[pelomai]] (to be busy, to trade); to barter (as a pedlar), that is, to sell:—sell, whatever is sold.

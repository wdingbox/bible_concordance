  #0850 αὐχμηρός auchmeros {owkh-may-ros'}

 from auchmos [probably from a base akin to that of G0109] (dust, as

—Greek Word Study (Transliteration-Pronunciation Etymology & Grammar)


 1) squalid, dirty
    1a) since dirty things are destitute of brightness: dark

—Thayer's (New Testament Greek-English Lexicon)

From αὐχμός [[auchmos]] (probably from a base akin to that of G0109; dust, as dried by wind); properly dirty, that is, (by implication) obscure:—dark.

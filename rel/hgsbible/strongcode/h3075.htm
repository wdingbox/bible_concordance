  #3075 יְהוֹזָבָד Y@howzabad {yeh-ho-zaw-bawd'}

 from H3068 and H2064; TWOT - n/a; n pr m

—Hebrew Word Study (Transliteration-Pronunciation Etymology & Grammar)


 Jehozabad = "Jehovah has endowed"
 1) son of Shomer, a Moabitish woman, who murdered Joash, king of Judah
 2) a Benjamite, captain of 180,000 armed men, in the days of
    Jehoshaphat
 3) a Korhite Levite, 2nd son of Obededom, and one of the porters of
    the temple and of the storehouse there in the time of David

—Brown-Driver-Briggs (Old Testament Hebrew-English Lexicon)

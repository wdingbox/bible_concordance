  #8254 שָׁקַל shaqal {shaw-kal'}

 a primitive root; TWOT - 2454; v

 1) to weigh, weigh out, pay out
    1a) (Qal)
        1a1) to weigh
        1a2) to weigh out (a price)
        1a3) of grief (fig)
    1b) (Niphal)
        1b1) to be weighed
        1b2) to be weighed out

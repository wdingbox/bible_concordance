  #2820 חָשַׂךְ chasak {khaw-sak'}

 a primitive root; TWOT - 765; v

—Hebrew Word Study (Transliteration-Pronunciation Etymology & Grammar)


 1) to withhold, restrain, hold back, keep in check, refrain
    1a) (Qal)
        1a1) to withhold, keep back, keep for oneself, keep from, hold
             in check, refrain, spare, reserve
        1a2) to restrain, check
    1b) (Niphal) to assuage, be spared

—Brown-Driver-Briggs (Old Testament Hebrew-English Lexicon)

A prim root; to restrain or (reflexively) refrain; by implication to refuse, spare, preserve; also (by interchange with H2821) to observe:—assuage, X darken, forbear, hinder, hold back, keep (back), punish, refrain, reserve, spare, withhold.

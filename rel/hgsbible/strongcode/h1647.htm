—Hebrew Word Study (Transliteration-Pronunciation Etymology & Grammar)


 Gershom = "foreigner"
 1) firstborn son of Moses and Zipporah
 2) firstborn son of Levi
 3) a son of the priestly family of Phinehas who returned from exile
    with Ezra

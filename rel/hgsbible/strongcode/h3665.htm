  #3665 כָּנַע kana` {kaw-nah'}

 a primitive root; TWOT - 1001; v

—Hebrew Word Study (Transliteration-Pronunciation Etymology & Grammar)


 1) to be humble, be humbled, be subdued, be brought down, be low,
    be under, be brought into subjection
    1a) (Niphal)
        1a1) to humble oneself
        1a2) to be humbled, be subdued
    1b) (Hiphil)
        1b1) to humble
        1b2) to subdue

—Brown-Driver-Briggs (Old Testament Hebrew-English Lexicon)

A primitive root; properly to bend the knee; hence to humiliate, vanquish:—bring down (low), into subjection, under, humble (self), subdue.

—Strong's (Hebrew & Chaldee Dictionary of the Old Testament)

  #2438 חִירָם Chiyram {khee-rawm'}
     or חִירוֹם Chiyrowm {khee-rome'}

 another form of H2361; TWOT - n/a; n pr m

—Hebrew Word Study (Transliteration-Pronunciation Etymology & Grammar)


 Hiram, Huram = "noble"
 1) the king of Tyre who sent workmen and materials to Jerusalem to
    build both the palace for David and the temple for Solomon
 2) the chief architect and engineer of Solomon's temple sent by
    King Hiram to Solomon

—Brown-Driver-Briggs (Old Testament Hebrew-English Lexicon)

Another form of H2361; Chiram or Chirom, the name of two Tyrians:—Hiram, Huram.

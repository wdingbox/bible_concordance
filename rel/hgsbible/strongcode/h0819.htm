  #0819 אַשְׁמָה 'ashmah {ash-maw'}

 from H0817; TWOT - 180c; n f

—Hebrew Word Study (Transliteration-Pronunciation Etymology & Grammar)


 1) guiltiness, guilt, offense, sin, wrong-doing
    1a) doing wrong, committing a trespass or offense
    1b) becoming guilty, guilt
    1c) bringing a guilt-offering

—Brown-Driver-Briggs (Old Testament Hebrew-English Lexicon)

Feminine of H0817; guiltiness, a fault, the presentation of a sin offering:—offend, sin, (cause of) trespass (-ing, offering).

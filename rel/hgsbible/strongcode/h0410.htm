  #0410 אֵל 'el {ale}

 shortened from H0352; TWOT - 93a; n m

—Hebrew Word Study (Transliteration-Pronunciation Etymology & Grammar)


 1) god, god-like one, mighty one
    1a) mighty men, men of rank, mighty heroes
    1b) angels
    1c) god, false god, (demons, imaginations)
    1d) God, the one true God, Jehovah
 2) mighty things in nature
 3) strength, power

—Brown-Driver-Briggs (Old Testament Hebrew-English Lexicon)

Shortened from H0352; strength; as adjective mighty; especially the Almighty (but used also of any deity):—God (god), X goodly, X great, idol, might (-y one), power, strong. Compare names in "-el."

  #0454 אֶלְיְהוֹעֵינַי 'Ely@how`eynay {el-ye-ho-ay-nah'ee}
   or (shortened) 'Elyow`eynay {el-yo-ay-nah'ee}

 from H0413 and H3068 and H5869; TWOT - n/a; n pr m

—Hebrew Word Study (Transliteration-Pronunciation Etymology & Grammar)


 Elioenai or Elihoenai = "unto Jehovah are my eyes"
 1) a Korahite temple doorkeeper, son of Meshelemiah
 2) two men with foreign wife during the exile; one a priest
 3) a son of Neariah
 4) a Simeonite
 5) a Benjamite, son of Becher

—Brown-Driver-Briggs (Old Testament Hebrew-English Lexicon)

From H0413 and H3068 and H5869; towards Jehovah (are) my eyes; Eljehoenai or Eljoenai, the name of seven Israelites:—Elihoenai, Elionai.

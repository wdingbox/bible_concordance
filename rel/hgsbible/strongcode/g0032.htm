  #0032 ἄγγελος aggelos {ang'-el-os}

 from aggello [probably derived from G0071, cf G0034] (to bring tidings);
    TDNT - 1:74,12; n m

—Greek Word Study (Transliteration-Pronunciation Etymology & Grammar)


 1) a messenger, envoy, one who is sent, an angel, a messenger
    from God

—Thayer's (New Testament Greek-English Lexicon)

From ἀγγέλλω [[aggello]] (probably derived from G0071; compare G0034; to bring tidings); a messenger; especially an "angel"; by implication a pastor:—angel, messenger.

 1) one of the peoples of east Canaan and beyond the Jordan, dispossessed

—Brown-Driver-Briggs (Old Testament Hebrew-English Lexicon)

Probably a patronymic from an unused name derived from H0559 in the sense of publicity, that is, prominence; thus a mountaineer; an Emorite, one of the Canaanitish tribes:—Amorite.

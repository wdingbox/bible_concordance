  #0014 אבה 'abah {aw-baw'}

 a primitive root; TWOT - 3; v

—Hebrew Word Study (Transliteration-Pronunciation Etymology & Grammar)


 1) to be willing, consent
    1a) (Qal)
        1a1) to be willing
        1a2) to consent, yield to, accept
        1a3) to desire

—Brown-Driver-Briggs (Old Testament Hebrew-English Lexicon)

A primitive root; to breathe after, that is (figuratively) to be acquiescent:—consent, rest content, will, be willing.

  #3664 כָּנַס kanac {kaw-nas'}

 a primitive root; TWOT - 1000; v

—Hebrew Word Study (Transliteration-Pronunciation Etymology & Grammar)


 1) to gather, collect, wrap
    1a) (Qal) to gather
    1b) (Piel) to gather
    1c) (Hithpael) to gather together, wrap oneself up

—Brown-Driver-Briggs (Old Testament Hebrew-English Lexicon)

A primitive root; to collect; hence, to enfold:—gather (together), heap up, wrap self.

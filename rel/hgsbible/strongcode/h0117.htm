  #0117 אַדִּיר 'addiyr {ad-deer'}

 from H0142; TWOT - 28b; adj

—Hebrew Word Study (Transliteration-Pronunciation Etymology & Grammar)


 1) great, majestic
    1a) of waters of sea
    1b) of a tree
    1c) of kings, nations, gods
 2) great one, majestic one
    2a) of nobles, chieftains, servants

—Brown-Driver-Briggs (Old Testament Hebrew-English Lexicon)

From H0142; wide or (generally) large; figuratively powerful:—excellent, famous, gallant, glorious, goodly, lordly, mighty (-ier, one), noble, principal, worthy.

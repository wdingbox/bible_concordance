  #1422 δύσκολος duskolos {doo'-kol-os}

 from G1418 and kolon (food); TDNT - n/a; adj

—Greek Word Study (Transliteration-Pronunciation Etymology & Grammar)


 1) hard to find agreeable food for, fastidious about food
 2) difficult to please, always finding fault
 3) difficult

—Thayer's (New Testament Greek-English Lexicon)

From G1418 and κόλον [[kolon]] (food); properly fastidious about eating (peevish), that is, (generally) impracticable:—hard.

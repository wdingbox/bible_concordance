  #0780 אֲרָרַט 'Ararat {ar-aw-rat'}

 of foreign origin; TWOT - 169; n pr loc

—Hebrew Word Study (Transliteration-Pronunciation Etymology & Grammar)


 Ararat = "the curse reversed: precipitation of curse"
 1) a mountainous region of eastern Armenia, between the river Araxes and
    the lakes Van and Oroomiah, the site where Noah's ark came to rest
 2) (TWOT) the mountain where Noah's ark came to rest

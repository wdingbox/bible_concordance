  #1361 גּבהּ gabahh {gaw-bah'}

 a primitive root; TWOT - 305; v

—Hebrew Word Study (Transliteration-Pronunciation Etymology & Grammar)


 1) to be high, be exalted
    1a) (Qal)
        1a1) to be high, lofty, tall
        1a2) to be exalted
        1a3) to be lofty
             1a3a) to be lofty (of Jehovah's ways - good sense)
             1a3b) to be haughty, be arrogant (bad sense)
    1b) (Hiphil) to make high, exalt

—Brown-Driver-Briggs (Old Testament Hebrew-English Lexicon)

A primitive root; to soar, that is, be lofty; figuratively to be haughty:—exalt, be haughty, be (make) high (-er), lift up, mount up, be proud, raise up great height, upward.

—Hebrew Word Study (Transliteration-Pronunciation Etymology & Grammar)


 Meribah = "strife" or "contention"
 1) a fountain at Rephidim, in the desert of Sin; so called because
    the Israelites murmured against God
 2) the name of the water supply at Kadesh on the southern border of
    the promised land; the people also murmured here against God

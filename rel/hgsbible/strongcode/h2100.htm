  #2100 זוּב zuwb {zoob}

 a primitive root; TWOT - 534; v

—Hebrew Word Study (Transliteration-Pronunciation Etymology & Grammar)


 1) to flow, gush, issue, discharge
    1a) (Qal)
        1a1) to flow, gush
        1a2) to die, pine away (fig.)
        1a3) to flow (of issue from woman), have an issue, flux
        1a4) flowing (participle)

—Brown-Driver-Briggs (Old Testament Hebrew-English Lexicon)

A primitive root; to flow freely (as water), that is, (specifically) to have a (sexual) flux; figuratively to waste away; also to overflow:—flow, gush out, have a (running) issue, pine away, run.

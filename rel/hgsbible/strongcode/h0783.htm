  #0783 אַרְתַּחשַׁשְׂתָּא 'Artachshashta' {ar-takh-shash-taw'}
     or אַרְתַּחשַׁשְׂת 'Artachshasht'
      {ar-takh-shasht'}; or by permutation 'Artachshact' {ar-takh-shast'}

 of foreign origin; TWOT - n/a; n pr m

—Hebrew Word Study (Transliteration-Pronunciation Etymology & Grammar)


 Artaxerxes = "I will make the spoiled to boil: I will stir myself (in) winter"
 1) son and successor of Xerxes as emperor of Persia, 465-424 BC

—Brown-Driver-Briggs (Old Testament Hebrew-English Lexicon)

Of foreign origin; Artachshasta (or Artaxerxes), a title (rather than name) of several Persian kings:—Artaxerxes.

 intensive from the same as H8565; TWOT - 2528b; n m

—Hebrew Word Study (Transliteration-Pronunciation Etymology & Grammar)


 1) dragon, serpent, sea monster

    1b) sea or river monster
    1c) serpent, venomous snake

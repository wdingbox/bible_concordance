  #3672 כִּנֶּרוֹת Kinn@rowth {kin-ner-oth'}
     or כִּנֶּרֶת Kinnereth {kin-neh'-reth}

 respectively pl. and sing. fem. from the same as H3658; TWOT - n/a; n pr loc

—Hebrew Word Study (Transliteration-Pronunciation Etymology & Grammar)


 Chinneroth or Cinneroth or Chinnereth = "harps"
 1) the early name of the Sea of Galilee
 2) a town and district in Naphtali near the Sea of Galilee

—Brown-Driver-Briggs (Old Testament Hebrew-English Lexicon)

Respectively plural and singular feminine from the same as H3658; perhaps harp shaped; Kinneroth or Kinnereth, a place in Palestine:—Chinnereth, Chinneroth, Cinneroth.

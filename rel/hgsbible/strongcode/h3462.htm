  #3462 יָשֵׁן yashen {yaw-shane'}

 a primitive root; TWOT - 928; v

—Hebrew Word Study (Transliteration-Pronunciation Etymology & Grammar)


 1) to sleep, be asleep
    1a) (Qal) to sleep, go to sleep, be asleep
    1b) (Niphal)
        1b1) to remain a long time, be stored up
        1b2) to be festering (of leprosy)
    1c) (Piel) to cause to sleep, entice to sleep

—Brown-Driver-Briggs (Old Testament Hebrew-English Lexicon)

A primitive root; properly to be slack or languid, that is, (by implication) sleep (figuratively to die); also to grow old, stale or inveterate:—old (store), remain long, (make to) sleep.

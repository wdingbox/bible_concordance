  #0853 אֵת 'eth {ayth}

 apparent contracted from H0226 in the demonstrative sense of
    entity; TWOT - 186; untranslated particle

—Hebrew Word Study (Transliteration-Pronunciation Etymology & Grammar)


 1) sign of the definite direct object, not translated in English
    but generally preceding and indicating the accusative

—Brown-Driver-Briggs (Old Testament Hebrew-English Lexicon)

Apparently contracted from H0226 in the demonstrative sense of entity; properly self (but generally used to point out more definitely the object of a verb or preposition, even or namely):—(As such unrepresented in English.)

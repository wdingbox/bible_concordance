  #7156 קִרְיָתַיִם Qiryathayim {keer-yaw-thah'-yim}

 dual of H7151; TWOT - n/a; n pr loc

—Hebrew Word Study (Transliteration-Pronunciation Etymology & Grammar)


 Kiriathaim = "two cities"
 1) a city east of the Jordan in Moab
 2) a town in Naphtali allotted to the Gershonite Levites

—Brown-Driver-Briggs (Old Testament Hebrew-English Lexicon)

Dual of H7151; double city; Kirjathaim, the name of two places in Palestine:—Kiriathaim, Kirjathaim.

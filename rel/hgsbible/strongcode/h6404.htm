—Hebrew Word Study (Transliteration-Pronunciation Etymology & Grammar)


 Pelet = "deliverance"
 1) son of Jahdai and a descendant of Caleb
 2) son of Azmaveth and one of David's mighty warriors who joined him
    at Ziklag

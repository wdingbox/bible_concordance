  #0430 אֱלֹהִים 'elohiym {el-o-heem'}

 plural of H0433; TWOT - 93c; n m p

—Hebrew Word Study (Transliteration-Pronunciation Etymology & Grammar)


 1) (plural)
    1a) rulers, judges
    1b) divine ones
    1c) angels
    1d) gods
 2) (plural intensive - singular meaning)
    2a) god, goddess
    2b) godlike one
    2c) works or special possessions of God
    2d) the (true) God
    2e) God

—Brown-Driver-Briggs (Old Testament Hebrew-English Lexicon)

Plural of H0433; gods in the ordinary sense; but specifically used (in the plural thus, especially with the article) of the supreme God; occasionally applied by way of deference to magistrates; and sometimes as a superlative:—angels, X exceeding, God (gods) (-dess, -ly), X (very) great, judges, X mighty.

  #6085 עֶפְרוֹן `Ephrown {ef-rone'}

 from the same as H6081;

—Hebrew Word Study (Transliteration-Pronunciation Etymology & Grammar)


 Ephron = "fawn-like"
 n pr m
 1) a Hittite, son of Zohar and the one from whom Abraham bought the
    field and cave of Machpelah
 n pr loc
 2) a city on the borders of Benjamin
 3) a mountain on the northern border of Judah

—Brown-Driver-Briggs (Old Testament Hebrew-English Lexicon)

From the same as H6081; fawn like; Ephron, the name of a Canaanite and of two places in Palestine:—Ephron, Ephrain [from the margin].

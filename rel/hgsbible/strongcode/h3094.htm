  #3094 יְהַלֶּלְאֵל Y@hallel'el {yeh-hal-lel-ale'}

 from H1984 and H0410; TWOT - n/a; n pr m

—Hebrew Word Study (Transliteration-Pronunciation Etymology & Grammar)


 Jehaleleel or Jehalelel = "God is praised"
 1) a Calebite descendant of Judah
 2) father of Azariah and a Merarite Levite in the time of Hezekiah

—Brown-Driver-Briggs (Old Testament Hebrew-English Lexicon)

From H1984 and H0410; praising God; Jehallelel, the name of two Israelites:—Jehaleleel, Jehalelel.

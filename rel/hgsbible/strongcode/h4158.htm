—Hebrew Word Study (Transliteration-Pronunciation Etymology & Grammar)


 Mephaath = "splendour" or "height"
 1) a city of Reuben allotted to the Merarite Levites lying in the
    district of the Mishor; site uncertain
 2) a city in Moab

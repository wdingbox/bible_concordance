  #7802 שׁוּשַׁן עֵדוּת Shuwshan `Eduwth {shoo-shan' ay-dooth'}
     or (pl. of former) שׁוֹשַׁנִּים עֵדוּת Showshanniym `Eduwth {sho-shan-neem' ay-dooth'}

 from H7799 and H5715; TWOT - n/a; n m

—Hebrew Word Study (Transliteration-Pronunciation Etymology & Grammar)


 Shoshannim-eduth or Shushan-eduth = "lilies"
 1) instruction in the title of Psalm 60 and Psalm 80
    1a) meaning uncertain

—Brown-Driver-Briggs (Old Testament Hebrew-English Lexicon)

The second form being plural; from H7799 and H5715; lily (or trumpet) of assemblage; Shushan Eduth or Shoshannim Eduth, the title of a popular song:—Shoshannim-Eduth, Shushan-eduth.

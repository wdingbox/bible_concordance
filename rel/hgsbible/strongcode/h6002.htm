  #6002 עֲמָלֵק `Amaleq {am-aw-lake'}

 probably of foreign origin; TWOT - n/a; n pr m

—Hebrew Word Study (Transliteration-Pronunciation Etymology & Grammar)


 Amalek = "dweller in a valley"
 1) son of Eliphaz by his concubine Timnah, grandson of Esau, and
    progenitor of a race of people in southern Canaan
 2) descendants of Amalek

—Brown-Driver-Briggs (Old Testament Hebrew-English Lexicon)

Probably of foreign origin; Amalek, a descendant of Esau; also his posterity and their country:—Amalek.

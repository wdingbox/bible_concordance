  #2282 θάλπω thalpo {thal'-po}

 probably akin to thallo (to warm); TDNT - n/a; v

—Greek Word Study (Transliteration-Pronunciation Etymology & Grammar)


 1) to warm, keep warm
 2) to cherish with tender love, to foster with tender care

—Thayer's (New Testament Greek-English Lexicon)

Probably akin to θάλλω [[thallo]] (to warm); to brood, that is, (figuratively) to foster:—cherish.

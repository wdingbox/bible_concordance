  #4328 προσδοκάω prosdokao {pros-dok-ah'-o}

 from G4314 and dokeuo (to watch); TDNT - 6:725,943; v

—Greek Word Study (Transliteration-Pronunciation Etymology & Grammar)


 1) to expect (whether in thought, in hope, or in fear)
 2) to look for, wait for

—Thayer's (New Testament Greek-English Lexicon)

From G4314 and δοκεύω [[dokeuo]] (to watch); to anticipate (in thought, hope or fear); by implication to await:—(be in) expect (-ation), look (for), when looked, tarry, wait for.

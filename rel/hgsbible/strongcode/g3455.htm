  #3455 μυκάομαι mukaomai {moo-kah'-om-ahee}

 from a presumed derivative of muzo ( to "moo"); TDNT - n/a; v

—Greek Word Study (Transliteration-Pronunciation Etymology & Grammar)


 1) to low, bellow, properly of horned cattle
 2) to roar, of a lion

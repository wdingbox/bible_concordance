     or (Jer 26:20) with the article interposed or (Jos 18:28) simply the former part of the word
     or קִרְיַת עָרִים Qiryath `Ariym {keer-yath' aw-reem'}

—Hebrew Word Study (Transliteration-Pronunciation Etymology & Grammar)


 Kirjath-jearim = "city of forests"
 1) a city on the northern boundary of Judah and on the western and
    southern boundaries of Benjamin
    1a) also 'Kirjath-baal' and 'Baalah'

—Brown-Driver-Briggs (Old Testament Hebrew-English Lexicon)

Used in Jeremiah 26:20 with the article interposed; or in Joshua 18:28 using simply the former part of the word; from H7151 and the plural of H3293 or H5892; city of forests, or city of towns; Kirjath Jearim or Kirjath Arim, a place in Palestine:—Kirjath, Kirjath-jearim, Kirjath-arim.

  #8271 שְׁרֵא sh@re' (Aramaic) {sher-ay'}

 a root corresponding to that of H8293; TWOT - 3048; v

—Hebrew Word Study (Transliteration-Pronunciation Etymology & Grammar)


 1) to loosen, abide, begin
    1a) (P'al)
        1a1) to loosen
        1a2) to abide (from loosing girths for camp)
    1b) (Pael) to begin, open
    1c) (Ithpael) to be loosened

—Brown-Driver-Briggs (Old Testament Hebrew-English Lexicon)

(Chaldee); a root corresponding to that of H8293; to free, separate; figuratively to unravel, commence; by implication (of unloading beasts) to reside:—begin dissolve, dwell, loose.

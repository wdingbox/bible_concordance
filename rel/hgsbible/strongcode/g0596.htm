  #0596 ἀποθήκη apotheke {ap-oth-ay'-kay}

 from G0659; TDNT - n/a; n f

—Greek Word Study (Transliteration-Pronunciation Etymology & Grammar)


 1) a place in which anything is laid by or up
 2) a storehouse, granary

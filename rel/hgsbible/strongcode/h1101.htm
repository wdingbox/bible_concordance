  #1101 בּלל balal {baw-lal'}

 a primitive root; TWOT - 248; v

—Hebrew Word Study (Transliteration-Pronunciation Etymology & Grammar)


 1) to mix, mingle, confuse, confound
    1a) (Qal)
        1a1) to mingle, confuse
        1a2) to mix
        1a3) to give provender, feed (animals)
    1b) (Hithpoel) to mix oneself (among others)
    1c) (Hiphil) to fade away

—Brown-Driver-Briggs (Old Testament Hebrew-English Lexicon)

A primitive root; to overflow (specifically with oil); by implication to mix; also (denominative from H1098) to fodder:—anoint, confound, X fade, mingle, mix (self), give provender, temper.

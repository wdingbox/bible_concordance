—Thayer's (New Testament Greek-English Lexicon)

Third person singular present indicative of a compound of G1537 and G1510; so also ἐξόν [[exon]], {ex-on'}; neuter present participle of the same (with or without some form of G1510 expressed); impersonally it is right (through the figurative idea of being out in public):—be lawful, let, X may (-est).

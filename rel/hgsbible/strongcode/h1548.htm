 a primitive root; TWOT - 351; v

 1) to poll, shave, shave off, be bald
    1a) (Piel)
        1a1) to shave
        1a2) to shave off
        1a3) (fig. of devastation)
    1b) (Pual) to be shaven

—Brown-Driver-Briggs (Old Testament Hebrew-English Lexicon)

A primitive root; properly to be bald, that is, (causatively) to shave; figuratively to lay waste:—poll, shave (off).

  #1632 ἐκχέω ekcheo {ek-kheh'-o}
     or (by variation) ἐκχύνω ekchuno {ek-khoo'-no}

 from G1537; TDNT - 2:467,220; v

—Greek Word Study (Transliteration-Pronunciation Etymology & Grammar)


 1) to pour out, shed forth
 2) metaph. to bestow or distribute largely

—Thayer's (New Testament Greek-English Lexicon)

From G1537 and χέω [[cheo]] (to pour); to pour forth; figuratively to bestow:—gush (pour) out, run greedily (out), shed (abroad, forth), spill.

  #3512 כָּאֶה ka'ah {kaw-aw'}

 a primitive root; TWOT - 941; v

—Hebrew Word Study (Transliteration-Pronunciation Etymology & Grammar)


 1) to be sad, be disheartened, be cowed
    1a) (Niphal) to be disheartened, be cowed

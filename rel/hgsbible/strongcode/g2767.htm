  #2767 κεράννυμι kerannumi {ker-an'-noo-mee}

 a prolonged form of a more primary kerao {ker-ah'-o} (which is
    used in certain tenses); TDNT - n/a; v

—Greek Word Study (Transliteration-Pronunciation Etymology & Grammar)


 1) to mix, mingle
 2) to mix wine, water
 3) to pour out for drinking.

—Thayer's (New Testament Greek-English Lexicon)

A prolonged form of a more primary word κεράω [[kerao]], {ker-ah'-o} (which is used in certain tenses); to mingle, that is, (by implication) to pour out (for drinking):—fill, pour out. Compare G3396.

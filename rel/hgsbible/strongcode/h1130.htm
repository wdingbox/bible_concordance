  #1130 בֶּן־הֲדַד Ben-Hadad {ben-had-ad'}

 from H1121 and H1908; TWOT - n/a; n pr m

—Hebrew Word Study (Transliteration-Pronunciation Etymology & Grammar)


 Ben-hadad = "son of [the false god] Hadad"
 1) the king of Syria, contemporary with Asa of Judah
 2) the son of Hazael, also king of Syria

—Brown-Driver-Briggs (Old Testament Hebrew-English Lexicon)

From H1121 and H1908; son of Hadad; Ben Hadad, the name of several Syrian kings:—Ben-hadad.

  #7644 שֶׁבְנָא Shebna' {sheb-naw'}: or שֶׁבְנָה Shebnah {sheb-naw'}

 from an unused root meaning to grow; TWOT - n/a; n pr m

—Hebrew Word Study (Transliteration-Pronunciation Etymology & Grammar)


 Shebna = "vigour"
 1) a person of high position in the court of king Hezekiah of Judah;
    subsequently the secretary of Hezekiah

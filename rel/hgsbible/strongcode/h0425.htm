  #0425 אֵלָה 'Elah {ay-law'}

 the same as H0424; TWOT - n/a; n pr m,loc

—Hebrew Word Study (Transliteration-Pronunciation Etymology & Grammar)


 Elah = "An oak"
 1) an Edomite chief
 2) a king of Israel for two years, son of Baasha
 3) the father of king Hoshea of Israel
 4) a son of Caleb
 5) son of Uzzi

—Brown-Driver-Briggs (Old Testament Hebrew-English Lexicon)

The same as H0424; Elah, the name of an Edomite, or four Israelites, and also of a place in Palestine:—Elah.

  #5118 τοσοῦτος tosoutos {tos-oo'-tos}

 from tosos (so much, apparently from G3588 and G3739) and G3778
    (including its variations); TDNT - n/a; adj

—Greek Word Study (Transliteration-Pronunciation Etymology & Grammar)


 1) of quantity: so great, so many
 2) of time: so long

—Thayer's (New Testament Greek-English Lexicon)

From τόσος [[tosos]] (so much; apparently from G3588 and G3739) and G3778 (including its variations); so vast as this, that is, such (in quantity, amount, number or space):—as large, so great (long, many, much), these many.

—Hebrew Word Study (Transliteration-Pronunciation Etymology & Grammar)


 n pr m
 Sheba = "seven"
 1) Benjamite, son of Bichri and one who led a rebellion against David
 2) Gadite, brother of Michael, Meshullam, Jorai, Jachan, Zia, and Heber
 n pr loc
 3) a town in Simeon

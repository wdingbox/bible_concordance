  #4125 מוֹאָבִי Mow'abiy {mo-aw-bee'} fem. Mow'abiyah {mo-aw-bee-yaw'}
   or Mowabiyth {mo-aw-beeth'}

 patronymical from H4124; TWOT - 1155a; adj

—Hebrew Word Study (Transliteration-Pronunciation Etymology & Grammar)


 Moabite = "from father: what father?"
 1) an citizen of Moab
 2) an inhabitant of the land of Moab

—Brown-Driver-Briggs (Old Testament Hebrew-English Lexicon)

Patronymic from H4124; a Moabite or Moabitess, that is, a descendant from Moab:—(woman) of Moab, Moabite (-ish, -ss).

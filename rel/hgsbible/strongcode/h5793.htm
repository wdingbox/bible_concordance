—Hebrew Word Study (Transliteration-Pronunciation Etymology & Grammar)


 Uthai = "helpful"
 1) son of Ammihud and a descendant of Pharez of Judah
 2) one of the sons of Bigvai who returned from exile with the 2nd
    caravan with Ezra
